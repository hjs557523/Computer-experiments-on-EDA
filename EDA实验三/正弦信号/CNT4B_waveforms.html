Sample behavioral waveforms for design file "CNT4B.v"

The following waveforms show the behavior of lpm_counter megafunction for the chosen set of parameters in design "CNT4B.v". The design "CNT4B.v" is a 4 bit dynamically controlled up/down modulus 12 counter. The counter supports a synchronous load of data input. The counter has an asynchronous clear input.

Fig. 1 : Wave showing counter operation.

The output port cout will be asserted at the completion of count sequence. The ports cin and cout are used to chain multiple counters together.
